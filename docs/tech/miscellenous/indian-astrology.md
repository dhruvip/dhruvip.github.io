**Books:**

* **Vedic Astrology for Beginners: An Introduction to the Origins and Core Concepts of Jyotish** by Comilla Sutton: This book provides a comprehensive introduction to the basic concepts of Vedic astrology, including the planets, signs, houses, and aspects. It is written in a clear and accessible style, making it a great starting point for beginners.
[Image of Vedic Astrology for Beginners: An Introduction to the Origins and Core Concepts of Jyotish by Comilla Sutton]
* **Light on Life: An Introduction to the Astrology of India** by Hart De Fouw: This book offers a more in-depth exploration of Vedic astrology, covering topics such as the dasha system, transits, and predictive techniques. It is written in a clear and engaging style, making it accessible to readers with a basic understanding of the subject.
[Image of Light on Life: An Introduction to the Astrology of India by Hart De Fouw]
* **The Art and Science of Vedic Astrology** by Richard Fish: This book provides a detailed overview of the various aspects of Vedic astrology, including the planetary positions, the twelve houses, and the dasha system. It is a comprehensive resource for those who want to learn more about this ancient science.
[Image of The Art and Science of Vedic Astrology by Richard Fish]
* **Vedic Astrology: Easy&Simple (Vedic Astrology for beginners)** by Anatoly Malakov: This book is a beginner-friendly guide to Vedic astrology, covering the basic concepts in a clear and concise manner. It is a great resource for those who are new to the subject and want to learn the basics quickly.
[Image of Vedic Astrology: Easy&Simple (Vedic Astrology for beginners) by Anatoly Malakov]

**Blogs:**

* **Vedic Astrology Blog:** This blog provides a wealth of information on Vedic astrology, covering a wide range of topics from the basics to advanced techniques. The blog is written in a clear and accessible style, making it easy to understand even for beginners.
[Image of Vedic Astrology Blog]
* **The Astrology Podcast:** This podcast features interviews with leading astrologers from around the world. It is a great way to learn about the different schools of thought in Vedic astrology and to hear from experts in the field.
[Image of The Astrology Podcast]
* **The Astrologer's Apprentice:** This blog is written by a practicing astrologer who shares her insights and experiences with her readers. It is a great resource for those who are interested in learning more about the practical applications of Vedic astrology.
[Image of The Astrologer's Apprentice]

In addition to these resources, there are many other books, blogs, and websites that can help you learn Vedic astrology. It is important to find resources that are written in a clear and accessible style and that are appropriate for your level of understanding. With a little effort, you can learn the basics of Vedic astrology and gain a deeper understanding of this ancient science.
